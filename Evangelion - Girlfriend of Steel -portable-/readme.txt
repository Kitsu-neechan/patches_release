how to use:
-----------
- get your copy of girlfriend of steel portable iso (CRC32: 4488CCC5)
- name the iso to ULJM-05456.iso
- put ULJM-05456.iso into the same folder with patchme.bat, xdelta.exe and gfos.eng.v1.00.xdelta
- run patchme.bat
- run check_result.sfv to verify that the patching was succesful. (patched iso CRC32 should be 5D9646D4)
- put gfos.eng.v1.00.iso on psp or load in emulator and play game.
